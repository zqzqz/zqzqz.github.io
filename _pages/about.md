About Me
======
I am a 2nd-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science and Engineering at the University of Michigan working with Professor [Z. Morley Mao](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~zmao/).
My research interest broadly includes system security, network security, and software engineering. My current research focuses on designing safety or security checking tools for computer systems (e.g., autonomous vehicles, industrial control systems).
